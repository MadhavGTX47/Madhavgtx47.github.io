⚠️ Warnings & Gridlock Info
⚽ Sunday Estadio Azteca Match Traffic Warning
Estadio Azteca is hosting a FIFA Round of 16 match on Sunday, July 5. Kickoff is at 6:00 PM CST (local Mexico City time). Severe traffic gridlock will affect the southern boroughs (Xochimilco and Coyoacán) starting midday. You must escape northwest by 12:30 PM at the latest to avoid gridlock. Avoid traveling south or toward Centro after 4:00 PM.
📺 Watch Locations in Roma Norte
Since Mexico is playing, the city atmosphere will be electric. Secure a table at a local restaurant, sports bar, or cantina in Roma Norte or Condesa by 5:30 PM to watch the 6:00 PM broadcast. If they win, walk immediately to the nearby Angel of Independence roundabout on Paseo de la Reforma to see the city celebrate!
🎒 Arena México Security Policies (Friday Lucha Libre)
Arena México enforces a strict clear bag policy. No backpacks or professional cameras are allowed inside. Leave all non-essential items in your room at the Hotel Geneve. Bring small Pesos cash (MXN) to tip the seat ushers. Doctores neighborhood is not safe at night; always Uber directly to the arena door and back.
⚠️ CNTE Reforma Protests
Teacher association (CNTE) blockades are common on Paseo de la Reforma. Before walking to the Angel of Independence or booking an Uber along Reforma, ask the reception staff at Hotel Geneve for active alerts.
💧 Water & Food Safety
Never drink tap water — use bottled water even for brushing teeth. Avoid raw vegetables washed in tap water at street stalls. Stick to busy, high-turnover taquerías. Carry Imodium and Pepto-Bismol as a precaution.
🚕 Uber/DiDi Safety Tips
Always verify the license plate and driver before entering. Sit in the front seat (less targetable). Share your trip with a contact via the app. Avoid hailing street taxis — they're unregulated. Pin your destination accurately.

🚇 Getting Around CDMX
Transport Tips
🚇 Metro · 5 MXN per ride
Cheapest transit. Buy a Tarjeta de Movilidad Integrada (TMI) at any Metrobús station — NOT sold at the airport. Trains get very crowded at rush hour. Keep belongings in front pocket.
🚕 Uber / DiDi · 80-300 MXN typical
Safest option for tourists. Always use the app, never street hail. Cash option available. DiDi often cheaper than Uber.
🚌 Metrobús · 6 MXN per ride
BRT system along major arteries. Requires TMI card. Useful for Insurgentes corridor and Reforma.
🚶 Walking · Roma / Condesa / Centro
These neighborhoods are very walkable. Watch for uneven sidewalks. Avoid walking alone in Doctores, Tepito, or unknown areas after dark.
